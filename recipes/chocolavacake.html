  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 6 ounces chocolate (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°F and grease four 4 oz ramekins with butter, then coat with sugar.
  2. Whisk together sugar and cornstarch
  3. In a double boiler, melth the chocolate and butter together
  4. Add chocolate mixture to the sugar cornstarch mixture and whisk, continue to wisk while adding eggs, egg yolks, and vanilla
  5. Fill the ramekins and bake for 13 to 16 minutes
  6. When the tops are set but still jiggle, remove from the oven and plate
